# Solid AI

What is the future of work going to look like? 

Imagine you're a spreadsheet entry clerk. YOur job is to write spreadsheets. 

But one day, there's a new program coming around that can write spreadsheets better than you can. You find it one day, making its copies, hundreds of times faster than you can. And before you know it, you're out of a job.
